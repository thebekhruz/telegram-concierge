## ðŸš« Anti-Frustration Principles

### Rule #1: Always Offer Human Escape Hatch
**Every flow must include**:
```
"Need to speak with a real person?
[Connect Me to Admissions]"
```

**Never**: Trap users in endless bot loops

---

### Rule #2: Acknowledge Limitations
**When bot can't help**:
```
"That's a great question, but I want to make sure you get the best answer.

I'm connecting you with [Admissions Officer Name] who can help.

They'll respond within 2 hours (business hours).

Your question: [Repeat their question]
Reference: #12345"
```

**Never**: Give wrong information or guess

---

### Rule #3: Respect User's Time
**Keep conversations SHORT**:
- Max 3 questions before delivering value
- Show progress indicator: "Step 2 of 3"
- Allow "Skip" buttons for optional questions

**Example**:
```
âœ… Good:
"What's your child's age? [Quick reply buttons: 3-5, 6-10, 11+]"

âŒ Bad:
"To help you better, I need to know your child's exact date of birth, current school, grade level, interests, learning style, and future goals. Please provide detailed information."
```

### Rule #4: Make It Feel Human
**Use personality**:
- âœ… "Great question!"
- âœ… "I'd love to help with that!"
- âœ… "You're going to love our IB program!"
- âŒ "Processing request..."
